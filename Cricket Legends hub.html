AB de Villiers 2

Achievements: De Villiers holds many batting records, including the world's fastest One Day International (ODI) 50, 100 and 150, the fastest Test century by a South African and the fastest Twenty20 International (T20I) 50 by a South African. He won 3 ICC ODI Player Of The Year Awards (2010, 2014, 2015).
